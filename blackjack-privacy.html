Privacy Policy for Blackjack App

Effective Date: August 15, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Blackjack ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our mobile application (the "App"). Your privacy is our top priority. Our application is designed to be a completely offline, private tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers or any third party. All data you enter remains exclusively on your device.

Information We Collect and Store (Locally on Your Device)

The App stores the following information on your local device to provide its features:

  • Game Statistics: The App tracks your game performance, including wins, losses, pushes, and other metrics. This data is stored in a history of your past sessions or "runs."
  • Game Settings: Your preferences, such as the number of decks, felt color, card back design, and audio settings, are saved locally.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored by the App is used solely for the following purposes, all of which occur locally on your device:

  • To display your current and historical game statistics.
  • To customize your gameplay experience according to your saved settings.
  • To allow you to reset your bankroll and start a fresh run while archiving previous stats.

Permissions

The App does not require any special permissions to function.

Data Sharing

We do not collect or share any of your data. All information generated by the App remains on your device.

Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
